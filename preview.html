Article Preview

One place for HISE blog drafts, rendered graphics, and preview links.

Open current article Open lab tools
This dashboard is meant to be served from the repo root. Run .\preview.ps1 -Open, then use http://127.0.0.1:8066/preview.html.

Article Projects

Article folders: articles/<project-id>/article.md, article.json, assets/, renders/.

New article: .\scripts\NewArticleProject.ps1 -Slug hise-my-post -Title "[Blog] My Post", then add it to articles/index.json.

Shortcut: double-click OPEN_ARTICLE_PREVIEW.cmd or run .\preview.ps1 -Open from PowerShell.
